College Boards

Explore the various boards that govern different aspects of college life.

Public Relations

Manages the application and admission process for new students.

Science and Technology

Oversees academic policies, curriculum development, and student academic performance.

Training and Placement

Addresses student conduct issues and enforces college regulations.

Financial Aid Board

Provides financial assistance and scholarship opportunities to students.

Extracurricular Activities Board

Supports and oversees student clubs, organizations, and events.

Athletics Board

Governs intercollegiate athletics and promotes physical well-being.

Social Justice Board

Advocates for diversity, inclusion, and social justice initiatives.
